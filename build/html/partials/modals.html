Slot Machine Design
Bluegreen Vacations, 2019
Slot machine built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Designed using the HTML Canvas element. Compiled into an executable application using Electron. Templatized the various elements for later designing ease.

Project began November 2018 and was delivered February 2019. In Las Vegas, it is used a customer engagement tool. I programmed various odds and mechanics to designate a winner. The business decided on mechanic wherein the odds begin with a 1 in 5 chance of winning. Upon losing, the odds reduce to 1 in 4, and so on and so fourth until the the odds of winning are 1 in 1, forcing a win. Upon winning, the odds reset to 1 in 5.
